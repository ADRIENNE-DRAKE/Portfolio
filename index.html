Hi! My name is Adrienne Drake and I'm a Web Developer


Since a child I have been interested in how things worked. I would take radios, game systems and toys apart to see their inner workings.
My mother even bought me a phone that was transparent one year and I would stare at it for hours wondering how all those little components combined allowed the phone to operate.

Fast forward to years later as technology continued to grow and expand, and the development of processors became more intricate, I found myself drawn to software and how the components were being communicated with.



With over 20 years of Customer service experience I am looking to transfer that same passion into
the web development industry.
